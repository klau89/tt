Our story

The anti-country-club

We got tired of courts you had to join a club to touch, coaches you had to book weeks out, and prices that treated a casual hit like a luxury. So we built the opposite.

Tennis Terminal is a self-serve practice room where anyone — beginners, families, rusty former players — can walk in, grab a bay, and just play. No membership to belong to. No one watching. Your court, your schedule.

It started with a simple frustration: wanting to hit for an hour after work and finding nowhere in the neighborhood that would just let you. We figured smart locks and app-controlled machines could run a great little court without a front desk — and pass the savings straight to you.

What we're about

Three things we won't compromise on

Welcoming

Everyone belongs here — every age, body type and skill level. No dress code, no gatekeeping, no side-eye.

Tech-forward

The tech does the boring parts — booking, unlocking, feeding balls — so you can just focus on playing.

Affordable

No membership, no coach you're forced to book. Pay for the time you play — that's the whole model.
